[ about ]

Who we are.

Dr. Alfred Duncan

Director [macroeconomic modeling, applied economics]

Alfred is Senior Lecturer at the University of Kent, and specialises in advanced macroeconomic modelling and economic theory.

Alfred brings a macro-level perspective to the practice, helping clients understand how economic conditions — interest rates, credit markets and labour market dynamics — interact with the more targeted policy questions at the heart of a project.

Alfred's academic expertise brings practical modeling tools together with a deep understanding of economic theory to generate actionable insights and advice.

Dr. Emma Gorman

Director [applied economics, policy evaluation]

Emma is Principal Research Fellow at the University of Westminster's Centre for Employment Research, specialising in applied economics, causal inference and policy evaluation.

Her work uses large survey and adminstrative datasets to answer the big questions in social science and economics.

She has delivered research and evaluation for government and policy bodies including the DWP, DfE, the Low Pay Commission and the Social Mobility Commission, and published in high-impact academic journals across economics, statistics and the social sciences.

[ services ]

What we do

Macroeconomic modeling

Advanced macroeconomic modeling - such as CGE abd DSGE - to gain a deeper insight into how economic shocks impact the broader economy.

Policy evaluation

Designing and delivering robust impact evaluations — including difference-in-differences, regression discontinuity designs and other quasi-experimental methods — to assess what's working and why.

Applied econometrics

Analyses of administrative, survey and linked datasets to answer specific policy or business questions, with clear, decision-ready reporting.

Expert review & advisory

Expert review of research and analysis drawing on academic peer-review experience.

Training & workshops

Practical training in economic modeling for analysts and researchers, tailored to your team's tools and goals.
